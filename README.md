# Design and Analysis of Hybrid Toroidal–Conventional Contra-Rotating Propeller System (CRP)

An advanced maritime/hydrodynamic propulsion project focused on engineering and validating a high-efficiency Hybrid Toroidal–Conventional Contra-Rotating Propeller (CRP) system. This project bridges complex fluid dynamics with structural optimization and rapid prototyping.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

* **Timeline:** Jan 2026 – May 2026
* **Domain:** Hydrodynamics / Maritime Propulsion
* **Core Methodologies:** Computer-Aided Design (CAD), Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Finite Element Analysis (FEA), and Additive Manufacturing.

Contra-rotating propeller systems offer improved aerodynamic/hydrodynamic efficiency by recovering residual rotational energy from the slipstream. This project introduces a **hybrid approach**, pairing toroidal blade geometry with standard conventional blades to significantly reduce tip vortex losses and acoustic signatures while maximizing thrust delivery.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools

* **Design & Modelling:** Autodesk Fusion 360
* **Simulation & Analysis:** ANSYS Fluent (CFD), ANSYS Workbench (FEA), SimScale
* **Prototyping:** Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) 3D Printing (ABS/PLA)

---

## 📈 Engineering Pipeline

### 1. Geometry Design (CAD)
* Engineered complex, highly precise blade profiles using **Autodesk Fusion 360**.
* Configured the dual-rotor contra-rotating hub mechanism to manage opposing rotational tracks cleanly.

### 2. Hydrodynamic Validation (CFD)
* Validated hydrodynamic fluid flow behavior using **ANSYS Fluent** and **SimScale**.
* Utilized the **SST (Shear Stress Transport) k-omega turbulence model** to capture near-wall flow characteristics and accurately evaluate wake interactions between the dual stages.
* *Key Metrics Monitored:* Thrust Coefficient ($C_T$), Torque Coefficient ($C_Q$), and overall propulsion efficiency ($\eta$).

### 3. Structural Integrity (FEA)
* Conducted static structural analysis via **ANSYS Workbench** to map stress concentrations.
* Ensured minimum structural displacement and verified factor of safety thresholds under peak hydrodynamic loading states.

### 4. Rapid Prototyping
* Successfully transitioned digital assets into functional physical components using **FDM 3D printing**.
* Produced physical models used for experimental visualization and geometric verification.
